# 🏦 BRIXS Stack Asset: Detailed Redemption & Utility Guide

Welcome to the **Brixs Chain Redemption Guide**. Brixs Chain is a Unified Liquidity L2/L3 tailored specifically for Emerging Markets (India, Africa, LatAm). By participating in our HACD Stack formation phase, you are helping bootstrap our decentralized network and securing your early allocation of BRIXS.

This document serves as the final, detailed guide on how to interact with, redeem, and utilize your BRIXS allocation.

## 🌟 1. Understanding Your BRIXS Formation

Unlike standard VC-funded token drops, **BRIXS is natively formed on the HACD Stack**. 

- **What you hold:** When you stake HAC to form BRIXS, you don't receive an arbitrary token in a wallet. You receive a **durable on-chain formation record** tied to a specific HACD.
- **The Proof:** This HACD acts as the Proof-of-Work container. It proves your exact formation time, the amount of HAC you spent (Stack Cost: 50 HAC per lot), and your lot allocation.
- **Supply Rules:** The total supply is strictly 1,000,000,000 BRIXS, formed across 1,000 HACD lots. 1 Lot = 1,000,000 BRIXS.

## 🛠️ 2. Testnet Utility: Gasless Account Abstraction

The immediate utility for your formed BRIXS is active participation in the **Brixs Chain Testnet L2**. We utilize Account Abstraction (AA) to offer gasless onboarding, crucial for developers and users in emerging markets.

### Step-by-Step Testnet Redemption
1. **Prepare Your Wallet:** Ensure you have access to the Hacash wallet that holds the HACD you used for the Stack formation.
2. **Connect to the Brixs Dashboard:** Navigate to our official Testnet portal (URL to be announced) and connect your wallet.
3. **Formation Verification:** The dashboard will ping the Hacash blockchain to read your Stack state. It verifies:
   - That you hold a valid stacked HACD.
   - Your exact timestamp of formation.
   - How many lots (and thus BRIXS) you are entitled to.
4. **Paymaster Whitelisting:** Once verified, your wallet address is automatically added to the **Brixs Paymaster Whitelist**.
5. **Transact Gaslessly:** You can now interact with the Brixs L2 Testnet. All transaction fees will be subsidized by the AA Paymaster up to your specific allocation tier.

## 🚀 3. Mainnet Redemption & Token Migration

When Brixs Chain successfully transitions from Testnet to Mainnet, your early formation records will serve as your claim ticket for real network utility.

### The Migration Process
- **The Snapshot:** Prior to Mainnet launch, a snapshot of the HACD Stack state will be taken. 
- **Token Claim:** On Mainnet, a smart contract will be deployed allowing you to claim your mapped BRIXS FT (Fungible Tokens). 
- **Conversion Rate:** 1 HACD Lot = 1,000,000 Mainnet BRIXS.
- **MEV Protection Rewards:** As an early participant, your wallet will be granted priority status in our MEV protection routing, ensuring you receive a percentage of MEV extracted from the network as an ongoing reward for your early faith in the ecosystem.

## ❓ 4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Do I need to "send" my HACD anywhere to redeem BRIXS?**
*No. Your HACD stays safely in your wallet. The Brixs dashboard only needs to read the public on-chain state to verify your Stack formation.*

**Q: Can I trade my BRIXS right now?**
*BRIXS is a utility Stack Asset, not a speculative investment. It is currently tied to your HACD formation record. Once Mainnet launches, the mapped FTs will be fully transferable.*

**Q: What happens if I un-stack my HACD?**
*If you un-stack your HACD before the Mainnet snapshot, you forfeit your formation record and will not be eligible for the Testnet Paymaster whitelisting or the Mainnet token claim.*
