# BRIXS Stack Asset: Redemption & Utility Guide

## Overview
BRIXS is a utility Stack Asset formed on the HACD Stack. By participating in the formation phase and staking HAC, you have secured a durable on-chain formation record. This guide explains how to redeem and utilize your BRIXS allocation within the Brixs Chain ecosystem.

## How to Redeem Your Utility
Your BRIXS formation receipt acts as your on-chain proof of participation.

1. **Connect Your Wallet:** Navigate to the Brixs testnet dashboard and connect the wallet containing your HACD Stack formation record.
2. **Verify Formation:** The dashboard will automatically read the HACD state and verify your formation timestamp and lot count.
3. **Activate Gasless L2:** Once verified, your wallet is whitelisted for the Brixs Account Abstraction (AA) paymaster.
4. **Transact:** You can now perform testnet L2 transactions with zero gas fees. The paymaster will subsidize your activity based on your participation tier.

## Future Mainnet Redemption
When Brixs Chain transitions from Testnet to Mainnet, your early formation records will be snapshotted.
- **Mainnet BRIXS:** Eligible wallets will be able to claim mainnet BRIXS tokens proportionally to their formed lots.
- **MEV Rewards:** Top-tier participants will be whitelisted for early MEV protection reward pools.

*Note: BRIXS is a utility token. Formation cost is non-refundable, and no price floor or financial return is guaranteed.*
